About Me

I am graduating with Ph.D. with Cybersecurity in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University (OSU) in Summer 2024. My advisors are Dr. Zhiqiang Lin and Dr. Yinqian Zhang. Before coming to OSU, I graduated from Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur (MNIT) with the Bachelor in Technology of Computer Engineering in 2013. My Bachelor theis project was supervised by Dr. Manoj Singh Gaur.

I am interested in research areas of automated formal verification, system security and privacy, trusted execution environment, cryptography, protocol design, and network security. I am actively looking for faculty positions in India to serve back in my country.
